The show starts off with the pirate kings execution where he says something that makes pirates from all over the country want to be like him. Then starts our story with a boy named Monkey D. Luffy that wants to set sail on an adventure to make his own crew and become the next pirate king. On his way doing so he makes comrades and turns even his enemies into allies when they all helped him in the Marineford War when he went to save his brother alongside the strongest pirate of the era Whitebeard. However along with his allies and his strength they were unable to save his brother Ace and he saw his brother's death right before his eyes. Due to this and as Luffy had potential the pirate kings right hand man Silvers Rayleigh ended up training Luffy over two years to teach him the stages of haki and make him stronger. After the two year time skip Luffy and his crew reunited and they set off to continue their adventure where they went into the New World and made more enemies coming up against tougher oppenents. The recent highlights of the post time skip is the fight to save the country of Dressrosa from an emperor of the sea Donquixote Doflamingo as he was using it's labs as a drug factory to duplicate devil fruits. After that is where Kaido and his crew are introduced but the story takes a turn to ano emperor of the sea, Big Mom where the crew goes to bring back the member Sanji and take down Big Mom as she was using Sanji's marriage as a way to attack the Vinsmoke family and take over their land. And with that, the adventure continues into the Wano Arc, where Luffy has been announced to become the fifth emperor of the sea after defeating Big Mom. The reason they're going to the Land of Wano is to challenge aanother emperor of the sea named Kaido, to put an end to his devil fruit trafficking business.

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main character of the series who is on an adventure with this crewmates to become the king of pirates. On his way he made new friends who have become his crewmates and they're called the Strawhat Pirates, on their way they face new challenges making enemies and new friends. So far he lost his brother in a war after he couldn't save him and trained with stronger pirates to become stronger in the gap of two years. Recently he beat an emperor of the sea Big Mom, also known as Charlotte Linlin and has acheived a bounty of 1.5 million beli and has been declared to be taking the spot as the fifth emperor of the sea. Now he's off to the country of Wano to meet up with his other crew members in the Wano as they make their strategy to defeat another emperor of the sea named Kaido. Luffy has the word 'D.' in his name which means danger as in other means they're called to be "God's Archenemy". He ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi devil fruits, which makes him immune to electric attacks, bullets and most blunt forces as well as giving him the ability to stretch his body at will.

Kaido also known as the world's "Strongest Creature" who is one of the current four emperor's of the sea. He is the leader of the Beasts Pirates which is his screw formed of ancient devil fruit users who transform into different creatures, an example being him, Kaido can transform into a dragon.
